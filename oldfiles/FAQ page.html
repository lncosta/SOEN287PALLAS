1.About Pallas
What is pallas?

Pallas is a webside also for performencer and for the clients. You can register to be a performer and to own an offcial page for present your performer background, past, recent and future events.You can also register to become an audience, seeking for different type of performance.

Why we choose pallas?

Unlike the other website, we introduce all the different type of performancers and client for their expectation, people who surf Pallas will have an opportunity to go through different kind of performances. You might at the beginning trying to see some performers that you already know and then find out some new group that you have never heard about and actually think it is interesting. Pallas opens a gate to all the performer to talk to each other, share different experience with performers in different domain. Combine all different culture together.

Who evented Pallas?

A group of students implement the Pallas idea for a course project. Luiza,Florian,Joseph, Piyush, Aline , and Yi Heng worked as a group to further developped the basic idea. However, there is a lot of space for improvement.

2.Account issue and personal information
Do we need an account?

It depends on the purpose of the user, if you are a performancer, it is mandatory to have an account in order to own a main page that you can post your own . For the audience, it is an option. If you want to get to know certain group in Pallas, you don't have to have an account. In the case that you want to buy a ticket for an event or buy certain groups accesorries, you don't have to creat an account, the receipt will be sent by email. However, it is very recommended to have an account. It will help you to track your order, your favorite event and performer, also, easier to contact us.

What information do I need for having an account?

You will have two different account types. Everyone will have same account which is pallas guest account at the beginning, it will ask your mandatory personal information such as name, emails, phone number, gender, and for easier to buy tickets, you can save your payment information at account field. If you would like to become a performer, you need to register for performer and fill out the performer information.

What should I do if I forget my password?

You can simply click "forget my password" button and answer safety questions, an email will be sent to your email to reset the password.

What should I do if somebody else use my account?

In the case that someone hack your account and post some posts, please contact the customer services to verify and delet actions. If the payment was made, please contact the bank for further information. We will never share our customer's information to the others, however, it is important to keep your account safe.

What if I am decide to close my account?

If you are an audience and you decide to use no more our website. You can write an email to delet your account. Once the account is deleted, no more information will stay on our system including your receipt, address, past history and so on. If you are a performer and decide to delet the account. You can submit an application through email. Your information include your performance inventory will stay in our system for 14 days, within the 14 days,you can cancel the application. Out the 14 days, all information will be deleted .

Can I set my account to private?

Of course you can set your account to private if you don't like any information shared. However, if you are a performer, once an event has been published and start ticket selling (even it is a free event), you need to write an email to the customer service to cancel the event.

3.Tickets purchase and sell
How can I buy a ticket?

You can either go to the ticket sales page or the page of the performance which you would like to watch.The payment will go through visa, mastercard, paypal. Some event will have an option to pay at the event. If there is this choice, you can check the box and pay at the event.

How can I sell ticket if I am a performer?

If you are a performer, just register for the event that on the performance page and click "The performance is not free" button, the information of the ticket price will appear and just fill the form, you will get the ticket price and the guests will be able to buy the tickets.The money will be hold by the website stakeholder until the event starts. After the events start, all fund will be deposit to the performer through legal bank bill transfer.

Can I have a physical ticket for souvenir?

Pallas recommended ticket sales online and there is an opportunity that people want to keep the physical(paper) ticket for their memory. It is depends on the performer. If the performer would like to serve paper ticket. It will be at the live event. Pallas will not offer mail service for it.

Can I sell my ticket for someone else?

You can sell ticket to someone else if you decide to not going. However, buy the tickets and sell it for profit will be prohibited. If the company find out that someone are buying huge amount tickets and sell it, it can be crutial.

4.Payment and refund
What kind of payment is acceptable?

Pallas will accept Master Card, Visa, Debit, and Paypal.

What if I regret about purchase the ticket?

Up to 48 hours before the performance begins, the ticket is refundable. Just click my order and click "cancel order" button, and the refund will be progress under normal circumstance. If within 48 hours before the performance begin, the ticket is not refundable.

How long it takes the refund?

The refund for the ticket will depend on the bank. Usually it takes 3~5 bussiness days. If you did not receive your refund within the time, please contact the customer services in order to make sure everything is going right. The refund will go to the orignal payment method.

Why my payment is unsuccessful?

There are many reason that the payment can be rejected. If the payment method is not wrong (such as the card information, the credit limit), there is a chance that the ticket is already sold out and there is a delay to the information. If you curious about why the payment is not successful, you can write an email to customer service, usually is takes 48 hours for an answer.
